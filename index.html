Welcome to Windy Hill Homestead! Our mission is to sustain our family from our land. This website is dedicated to documenting the development of our 20 acre farm and sharing what it is like to homestead in the High Desert.

We focus on pasture raised lamb, implementing practices to conseve water by planting Native Arizona seeds. Our permaculture orchard features three varieties of apples and two Native American plum varieties.

Each October we welcome our friends in the White Mountians to come to our U-Pick Orchard Days! For more information about the varieties we offer check out our orchard.
